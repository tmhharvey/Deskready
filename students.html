How Our Program Works
#1

Complete your DeskReady application on the “Sign Up” tab. Once completed, we will contact you for an interview with a DeskReady Instructor.
#2

The DeskReady experience begins! You will take part in an interactive cohort learning all you need to succeed in Sales and Recruiting. Here you will put your skills to the test while earning money.
#3

Here we will identify your industries of interest, earning goals and career aspirations. Working alongside our Partner Companies, we will set up interviews and help you take the RIGHT first steps into your Sales or Recruiting career.

#4

Congrats you made it through the DeskReady experience, now the fun begins! Equipped with a winning skillset, real industry experience, and a career you hand picked you are officially Desk Ready.

Our Training will help you Master:

Sourcing skills that enable you to generate high quality leads.
Prospecting techniques that consistently bring in new customers.
Running question based Discovery Calls that lead to deeper value.
Swiftly moving through objections to find commonality and impact.
Cutting edge software that streamlines your process and success.
Engaging your customer through empathetic selling and active listening.
A winning mindset needed for a successful career in sales or recruiting.
Closing strategies that get you the deal.
Your Options

Choose what payment option is best for you

Upfront Payment

$5,000 upfront or Two installments of $2,500

Income Share Agreement (ISA)

$0 upfront tuition. Enroll in our ISA to fund the cost of the course.

You only pay us 10% of your income when you are making above $40,000 a year. ISA cost is capped at $10,000 over 4 years.

Income Share Agreement (ISA) + Living Stipend

$0 upfront tuition. We provide you a $2,000 living stipend. Enroll in our ISA to fund the cost of the course.

You only pay us 10% of your income when you are making above $40,000 a year. ISA cost is capped at $14,000 over 4 years.
You may be liable for partial tuition if you drop out of class after enrolling. Those terms are outlined clearly in the Enrollment Agreement you receive once admitted, and our admissions advisors are available to help you understand them clearly.
