The Program

DeskReady students go through a comprehensive sales and recruiting training program, while earning an income in our Sales Lab. This is followed by graduation and career placement. How we get you DeskReady:

Group Lessons

Students will take part in live group lessons where they will collaborate with their cohort and instructors at a personal level. These lessons include content discussions, interactive activities and role plays that will develop your comfortability with finding and closing deals. This is a safe and engaging environment where we cherish developing new skills, building networks and making friends along the way.

1-on-1 Coaching

We understand that each DeskReady student learns at a different pace, in different ways and has different levels of experience. DeskReady’s 1-on-1 coaching provides a specific time for fostering individual growth and ensuring that each student's questions and considerations are sufficiently met so they can succeed. This time is often spent in role plays, diving deeper into course content, live outreach with your coach and career pathing. Let us personally give you the playbook to your success and focus on developing your individual and unique style.

The Sales Lab

The DeskReady Sales Lab is the real world experience you have been searching for. You will be a fully functioning sales rep and recruiter at DeskReady, working your own deals to close while receiving coaching from your manager. Hone your craft in a learning focused environment while building confidence in the skills you have developed. Oh… did we mention you will be getting paid?

Your Career

Your next career starts here! Aside from the skills and experience you will gain, DeskReady’s goal is to provide you with the sales or recruiting career you aspire for. We provide you interview training, resume building, and tailored career pathing to ensure that you have a leg up on the competition and life. Ready to start your career on the right foot?
